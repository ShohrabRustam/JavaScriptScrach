Synchronous VS Asynchronous

Synchronous

If i give a task with only one hand so I have to complete the task on by one it means one task at a time, this is the concept of the Synchronous [Follwing the Order](Dependency is there)

Asynchronous

If i give a task with only many hand so I have to complete the task depend in hands it means many tasks at a time, this is the concept of the Synchronous[May or may not follow order](Independency is there)
